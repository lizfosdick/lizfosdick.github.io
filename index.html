Welcome to Confetti Goods!

I'm Liz and this is my small business, based in Airdrie, Alberta. I've been working with epoxy resin since 2021 and UV resin since 2023. My specialities are stud earrings and Christmas ornaments! I sell my earrings on Etsy and a selection of products in the Squiggly Pig, a permanent market here in Airdrie. I am aiming to be at at least one holiday market in the Calgary area in 2024.
